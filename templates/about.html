Our Mission

Shop tomorrow's bestsellers today

TrendSphere was built as an M.Sc. Data Science capstone project at Symbiosis Skills & Professional University, Pune. Our ML pipeline uses three powerful models working in ensemble to forecast product demand with industry-beating accuracy.

We analyze Amazon India sales data, customer behavior, seasonal patterns, and social signals to predict what's going to be the next big trend — 30 days before it happens.
